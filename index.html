Employee Attrition Analysis Using Excel and Power Bi

A major company wanted to uncover the factors that lead to employee attrition.
I performed data cleaning using Microsoft Excel and extracted, transformed, loaded,
and modelled the data using Microsoft Power Bi, after which I created an interactive dashboard
that gave insights on the rate of attrition by gender, job role, work life balance, salary bracket and education.
At the end of the project, recommendations were made to decrease attrition rate and increase retention rate.
